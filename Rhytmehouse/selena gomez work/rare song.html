Selena Marie Gomez is an American actress, singer, producer, and businesswoman. Gomez began her career as a child actress, appearing on the children's television series Barney & Friends, and emerged as a teen idol for her leading role as Alex Russo on the Disney Channel sitcom Wizards of Waverly Place.
